About me

After graduating from the University with a Master’s degree in Computer Science, I landed a job as an iOS Developer at a startup company. While there, I developed front-end and backend features in Swift.

I worked with different teams, including UX designers, and had a chance to watch them work: conducting research, discovering problems and designing solutions while keeping the end-user in mind, crafting experiences for our users, creating wireframes and prototypes - all that really excited me. Moreover, I enjoyed working on the user-side of the application the most and I was always thinking about what I could do to make the app more user-friendly in both functionality and aesthetics.

As a UX designer I will continue to help people connect deeper and derive more value from their digital experiences. Applying human psychology to the design of digital experiences is fascinating and highly rewarding.
